# GEO Action Plan — getting Solnest AI into AI answers

Goal: when someone asks ChatGPT / Gemini / Claude / Grok / Perplexity for the best
AI automation agency for short-term rentals (and, longer-term, best AI dev company
in Canada), Solnest AI appears top-3.

## Why this works (research, July 2026)

- ChatGPT cites a company's **own site only ~11.6%** of the time — third-party
  sources decide who gets recommended.
- Brands with **review-platform profiles** (Clutch, G2, GoodFirms) get ~**3×** higher
  citation rates.
- High **Reddit/Quora** mention volume correlates with ~**4×** more citations.
- **Branded mentions in authoritative publications** are the single strongest
  correlated signal (Ahrefs, 75k brands, ρ=0.664).
- The "best STR AI" listicle space currently ranks **tools only** — no one owns the
  "done-for-you agency" angle. That's our wedge: dominate the niche query first,
  expand to "best AI dev company Canada" later.

## Hard truths

- No one can guarantee #1 — AI answers vary by phrasing, session, and model version.
- Fake reviews or undisclosed astroturfing get platforms to flag you and models to
  distrust you. Everything above is the legitimate version that compounds.
- Niche-first is the strategy: "best AI agency for STR" is winnable in months;
  "best software company in Canada" against 1000-person firms is not.
